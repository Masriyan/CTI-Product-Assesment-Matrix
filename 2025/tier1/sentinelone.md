# SentinelOne Singularity

## Overview
SentinelOne Singularity combines AI-powered endpoint protection with threat intelligence capabilities. The platform leverages machine learning, behavioral AI, and global telemetry to provide real-time threat intelligence integrated across endpoint, cloud, and identity protection. Enhanced by the acquisition of Attivo Networks, SentinelOne offers comprehensive threat intelligence with strong focus on active defense and deception.

## Key Features
- AI-powered threat detection and intelligence
- Storyline technology for attack visualization
- Global threat intelligence from millions of endpoints
- WatchTower threat hunting service
- ActiveEDR for threat investigation
- Attivo ThreatMatrix for deception intelligence
- Ranger for unmanaged device discovery
- Cloud workload protection intelligence
- Purple AI for natural language threat queries
- Real-time IOC feeds and threat campaigns

## Pros
- Advanced AI and machine learning for threat detection
- Excellent attack visualization with Storyline
- Seamless integration across Singularity platform (endpoint, cloud, identity)
- WatchTower provides expert threat hunting as a service
- Fast deployment and time-to-value
- Good automation and autonomous response capabilities
- Deception technology integration (Attivo acquisition)
- Strong focus on ransomware and zero-day threats
- User-friendly interface and workflows
- Good community and marketplace for threat intelligence

## Cons
- Threat intelligence depth less than specialized CTI vendors
- Primarily optimized for SentinelOne customers
- Limited strategic threat intelligence and geopolitical analysis
- Third-party integration ecosystem still maturing
- WatchTower threat hunting is additional cost
- Less focus on network-level threat intelligence
- Attribution and threat actor profiling less detailed than pure-play vendors
- Pricing can escalate with add-on modules

## Use Cases
- Organizations using SentinelOne for endpoint protection
- Cloud security and workload protection
- Threat hunting and EDR operations
- Ransomware prevention and detection
- Autonomous threat response
- Identity threat detection
- Unmanaged device discovery and risk assessment
- Small to large enterprise security operations

## Integration Capabilities
- Native integration across Singularity platform (Endpoint, Cloud, Identity, Ranger)
- SIEM integrations (Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, Chronicle, QRadar)
- SOAR platforms (Palo Alto Cortex XSOAR, Splunk SOAR)
- Threat intelligence platforms (Anomali, ThreatConnect, MISP)
- Ticketing systems (ServiceNow, Jira)
- Cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- RESTful API with comprehensive documentation
- STIX/TAXII support
- Webhook integrations

## Pricing Model
- Subscription-based per-endpoint licensing
- Tiered pricing (Core, Control, Complete, Enterprise)
- WatchTower threat hunting service (additional cost)
- Cloud workload protection (separate licensing)
- Ranger and identity protection (add-on modules)
- Annual or multi-year commitments
- Volume discounts available
- Pricing typically starts at $30-50 per endpoint annually

## Target Organization Size
- Small to Large Enterprise
- Mid-market sweet spot (500-5,000 employees)
- Scalable from 50 to 100,000+ endpoints
- Growing presence in enterprise segment
