# IBM X-Force Threat Intelligence

## üìã Overview

**Vendor:** IBM Corporation
**Headquarters:** Armonk, New York, USA
**Founded:** 1911 (X-Force established 1997, one of the industry's oldest threat intel teams)
**Website:** https://www.ibm.com/security/xforce

IBM X-Force Threat Intelligence represents one of the longest-standing and most respected threat intelligence operations in cybersecurity, with over 25 years of continuous threat research and analysis. Built on IBM's massive global security infrastructure monitoring 150+ billion security events daily across 130+ countries, X-Force delivers intelligence derived from real-world incident response engagements, vulnerability research by the elite X-Force Red penetration testing team, and decades of accumulated threat data. The platform uniquely integrates IBM Watson AI capabilities for advanced threat analysis and pattern recognition, while maintaining deep integration with IBM's comprehensive security portfolio including QRadar SIEM, Resilient SOAR, and Cloud Pak for Security. X-Force Exchange, IBM's community threat intelligence sharing platform, democratizes access to basic intelligence while premium tiers provide enterprise-grade intelligence tailored to specific industries. With a heritage spanning multiple threat eras from early viruses to modern ransomware, X-Force brings historical depth and institutional knowledge unmatched by newer platforms.
