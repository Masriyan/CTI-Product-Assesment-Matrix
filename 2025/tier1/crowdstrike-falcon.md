# CrowdStrike Falcon Intelligence

## Overview
CrowdStrike Falcon Intelligence is the threat intelligence arm of CrowdStrike, integrated within the Falcon platform. It combines threat intelligence research with endpoint protection, delivering adversary-focused intelligence derived from CrowdStrike's extensive telemetry data from millions of endpoints worldwide.

## Key Features
- Adversary-focused threat intelligence (170+ threat actor groups tracked)
- Integration with Falcon endpoint protection platform
- Automated indicator management and blocking
- Vulnerability intelligence and exploit monitoring
- Dark web monitoring and credential exposure alerts
- Malware analysis and sandbox detonation
- Custom intelligence reports and briefings
- Threat actor playbooks and TTPs
- Real-time intelligence feeds

## Pros
- Seamless integration with CrowdStrike Falcon EDR/XDR platform
- High-quality adversary attribution and tracking
- Real-time automated response to threats
- Excellent visibility into eCrime and nation-state actors
- Direct telemetry from millions of protected endpoints
- Strong malware intelligence and analysis capabilities
- Automated indicator enrichment within Falcon console
- Fast time-to-value for existing Falcon customers
- Comprehensive ransomware intelligence
- Regular intelligence briefings and webinars

## Cons
- Best value for existing CrowdStrike Falcon customers
- Limited standalone offering outside Falcon ecosystem
- Intelligence primarily optimized for endpoint threats
- Premium add-on pricing for advanced intelligence modules
- May have less depth in network-level threat intelligence
- Some overlap with EDR telemetry for existing customers
- Requires CrowdStrike expertise to maximize value

## Use Cases
- Organizations using CrowdStrike Falcon for endpoint protection
- Threat hunting and incident response teams
- Security operations centers focused on endpoint security
- Ransomware defense and prevention
- eCrime and financially motivated threat tracking
- Vulnerability prioritization and exploit monitoring
- Executive and VIP protection
- Threat actor attribution and profiling

## Integration Capabilities
- Native integration with CrowdStrike Falcon platform (EDR, XDR, Complete)
- Falcon Fusion SOAR workflow automation
- SIEM integrations (Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, Chronicle)
- Threat intelligence platforms (Anomali, ThreatConnect, ThreatQ)
- MISP and OpenCTI integration
- Third-party SOAR platforms
- RESTful API with comprehensive documentation
- STIX/TAXII feed support
- Webhook notifications

## Pricing Model
- Module-based pricing within Falcon platform
- Subscription pricing (annual or multi-year contracts)
- Tiered based on number of endpoints and intelligence modules
- Falcon Intelligence Premium for advanced capabilities
- Intelligence Recon for dark web monitoring (separate add-on)
- Custom enterprise pricing
- Bundled pricing with Falcon Complete or Falcon XDR

## Target Organization Size
- Small to Large Enterprise
- Particularly suited for existing Falcon platform customers
- Scalable from 100 to 100,000+ endpoints
