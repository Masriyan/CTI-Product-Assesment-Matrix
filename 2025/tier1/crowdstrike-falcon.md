# CrowdStrike Falcon Intelligence

## üìã Overview

**Vendor:** CrowdStrike, Inc.
**Headquarters:** Austin, Texas, USA
**Founded:** 2011
**Website:** https://www.crowdstrike.com/

CrowdStrike Falcon Intelligence is the threat intelligence component of the industry-leading CrowdStrike Falcon cybersecurity platform. Built on adversary-focused intelligence derived from protecting millions of endpoints globally, Falcon Intelligence delivers real-time threat data, adversary attribution, and automated response capabilities. The platform tracks 170+ named threat actor groups (APTs and eCrime) with detailed adversary profiles, TTPs, and targeting intelligence. What sets CrowdStrike apart is the tight integration between threat intelligence and endpoint protection, enabling automated defensive actions based on fresh intelligence. CrowdStrike's elite intelligence team, including veterans from government agencies, combines human expertise with AI-powered analytics to deliver actionable intelligence optimized for endpoint defense.

### Technical Challenges
- ‚úó **Endpoint-Centric Focus**: Intelligence optimized for endpoint threats; less depth in network-level or cloud-native threats
- ‚úó **Platform Dependency**: Full value requires CrowdStrike Falcon platform; standalone offering limited

- ‚úó **Premium Pricing**: Intelligence modules add significant cost on top of Falcon platform subscription

- Professional services and training

- Intelligence included in some Falcon XDR tiers
- Volume discounts for large endpoint deployments
