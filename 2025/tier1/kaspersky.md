# Kaspersky Threat Intelligence

## Overview
Kaspersky Threat Intelligence services are powered by the Kaspersky Security Network (KSN) and one of the industry's most respected research teams (GReAT - Global Research and Analysis Team). With over 20 years of threat research experience and telemetry from hundreds of millions of endpoints, Kaspersky provides comprehensive threat intelligence focused on advanced persistent threats, cybercrime, and targeted attacks.

## Key Features
- Kaspersky Security Network (KSN) with 400+ million users
- Global Research and Analysis Team (GReAT) elite research
- APT and targeted attack intelligence
- Cybercrime and underground economy monitoring
- Financial threat intelligence (targeted at financial sector)
- ICS/SCADA and industrial threat intelligence
- Malware analysis and reverse engineering
- YARA rules and IOC feeds
- Threat Data Feeds (IP, URL, File Hash)
- Dark web monitoring and attribution

## Pros
- World-class APT research from GReAT team
- Exceptional threat actor tracking and attribution
- Strong focus on nation-state and targeted attacks
- Excellent malware analysis and reverse engineering capabilities
- Leading ICS/SCADA and industrial cybersecurity intelligence
- Comprehensive financial threat intelligence
- High-quality YARA rules and detection content
- Regular in-depth threat research publications
- Strong presence in APAC, EMEA, and emerging markets
- Detailed threat reports with technical analysis

## Cons
- Geopolitical concerns and regulatory restrictions in some countries (US, UK bans)
- Primarily valuable for organizations facing sophisticated threats
- Best integration within Kaspersky product ecosystem
- Pricing at premium tier
- May be overkill for organizations facing commodity threats
- Third-party integration capabilities moderate
- Regulatory compliance challenges in certain jurisdictions
- Limited presence in US market due to government restrictions

## Use Cases
- Organizations facing advanced persistent threats
- Critical infrastructure and industrial control systems
- Financial services and banking sector
- Telecommunications providers
- Government agencies (in permitted jurisdictions)
- Threat hunting and incident response teams
- Security research organizations
- Malware analysis and reverse engineering teams

## Integration Capabilities
- Native integration with Kaspersky security products (EDR, KATA, Anti-Targeted Attack)
- Kaspersky Endpoint Security integration
- SIEM integrations (limited third-party)
- Threat intelligence platforms (ThreatConnect, Anomali, MISP)
- RESTful API and data feeds
- STIX/TAXII support
- YARA rule integration
- OpenIOC format support

## Pricing Model
- Subscription-based licensing
- Tiered pricing based on intelligence services (Data Feeds, Reports, APT Intelligence)
- Per-feed pricing for Threat Data Feeds
- Custom enterprise pricing
- Additional costs for GReAT analyst support
- Annual or multi-year contracts
- Pricing varies by region and regulatory environment
- Typically starts at $50,000+ annually for comprehensive intelligence

## Target Organization Size
- Mid-market to Large Enterprise
- Critical infrastructure organizations
- Government agencies (in permitted countries)
- Organizations with 1,000+ employees
- Particularly strong in EMEA, APAC, and LATAM regions
