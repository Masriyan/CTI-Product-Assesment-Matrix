# Trellix Threat Intelligence

## Overview
Trellix (formed from the merger of McAfee Enterprise and FireEye products) combines decades of endpoint security telemetry with FireEye's elite threat intelligence and incident response expertise. The platform provides comprehensive threat intelligence leveraging data from millions of sensors and frontline breach investigations.

## Key Features
- Advanced Threat Research team intelligence
- Telemetry from 1 billion+ sensors globally
- FireEye Mandiant integration for APT intelligence
- Real-time threat campaigns and IOC feeds
- Malware analysis and sandbox detonation
- Email threat intelligence
- File reputation and prevalence data
- MITRE ATT&CK mapped threat intelligence
- Automated indicator sharing across Trellix portfolio
- Vulnerability and exploit intelligence

## Pros
- Combination of McAfee's scale and FireEye's expertise
- Strong malware intelligence from massive sensor network
- Excellent integration within Trellix product ecosystem
- Access to APT research from former FireEye teams
- Good email threat intelligence from email security products
- Automated threat prevention across endpoints and network
- Strong file reputation database
- Regular threat research publications and reports
- Good coverage of commodity and targeted threats
- Integration with Google Mandiant intelligence

## Cons
- Platform integration still maturing post-merger
- Best value for customers using Trellix security products
- Some legacy product overlap and complexity
- Intelligence quality varies between product lines
- Pricing can be complex across merged portfolios
- Third-party integrations less mature than competitors
- Organizational changes may affect service consistency

## Use Cases
- Organizations using Trellix (McAfee/FireEye) security products
- Endpoint threat detection and response
- Email security and anti-phishing operations
- Enterprise threat intelligence programs
- Malware analysis and reverse engineering
- Incident response and threat hunting
- Network security monitoring
- Security operations centers

## Integration Capabilities
- Native integration across Trellix product portfolio (ENS, EDR, NX, Email Security)
- Trellix ePolicy Orchestrator (ePO) central management
- SIEM integrations (Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, IBM QRadar)
- Limited third-party TIP integration
- RESTful API access
- STIX/TAXII support
- Active Response integration for automated remediation
- Data Exchange Layer (DXL) for cross-product communication

## Pricing Model
- Subscription-based licensing
- Tiered pricing based on Trellix product deployment
- Threat intelligence often bundled with endpoint or network products
- MVision platform subscription
- Custom enterprise pricing
- Additional costs for premium threat intelligence feeds
- Pricing varies significantly based on product mix

## Target Organization Size
- Mid-market to Large Enterprise
- Organizations with 1,000+ employees
- Best suited for existing McAfee or FireEye customers
- Government and regulated sectors
