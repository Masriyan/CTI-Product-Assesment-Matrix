# Outpost24 Cyber Threat Intelligence (formerly Blueliv)

## Overview
Outpost24 Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI), incorporating the former Blueliv platform, provides external threat intelligence, digital risk protection, and attack surface management. Now part of the broader Outpost24 portfolio, the CTI solution combines threat intelligence from underground sources with vulnerability management and external attack surface monitoring to help organizations proactively defend against targeted threats.

## Key Features
- External threat intelligence platform
- Dark web and underground monitoring
- Attack surface discovery and management
- Targeted attack prediction
- Malware intelligence and campaigns
- Phishing and brand protection
- Credential leak detection
- Threat actor and campaign tracking
- Integration with Outpost24 vulnerability management
- Automated threat scoring and prioritization

## Pros
