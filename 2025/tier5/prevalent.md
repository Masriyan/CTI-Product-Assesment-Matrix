# Prevalent

## Overview
Prevalent is a third-party risk management and vendor risk assessment platform that combines security questionnaires, continuous monitoring, and threat intelligence to help organizations manage vendor and supply chain cyber risks. While not a pure-play threat intelligence platform, Prevalent integrates external threat intelligence and attack surface monitoring to provide comprehensive third-party risk visibility.

## Key Features
- Third-party risk management (TPRM) platform
- Automated vendor risk assessments
- Security questionnaire automation
- Continuous external monitoring
- Threat intelligence integration
- Attack surface visibility for vendors
- Risk scoring and prioritization
- Compliance and regulatory mapping
- Vendor risk reporting and dashboards
- Fourth-party (vendor's vendor) risk assessment

## Pros
- Comprehensive third-party risk management capabilities
- Good automation of vendor assessment workflows
- Continuous monitoring without vendor participation
- Strong compliance and regulatory mapping
- User-friendly questionnaire automation
- Good integration with GRC platforms
- Vendor collaboration portal
- Fourth-party risk visibility
- Strong in healthcare and financial services
- Regular risk intelligence updates

## Cons
- Primarily TPRM platform, not comprehensive CTI
- Limited tactical threat intelligence features
- Best suited for vendor risk vs. operational security
- Integration with security operations tools limited
- Can be expensive for comprehensive vendor portfolios
- Some customers report questionnaire fatigue
- API capabilities moderate

## Use Cases
- Third-party and vendor risk management
- Supply chain cyber risk assessment
- Vendor onboarding and due diligence
- Regulatory compliance (DORA, NYDFS, FFIEC)
- Healthcare third-party risk (HIPAA)
- Financial services vendor management
- M&A due diligence
- Continuous vendor monitoring

## Integration Capabilities
- GRC platforms (ServiceNow GRC, Archer, OneTrust, NAVEX)
- Procurement systems (Coupa, Ariba)
- Risk management platforms
- Ticketing (ServiceNow, Jira)
- SIEM integration (limited)
- RESTful API
- SSO integration (SAML)
- Business intelligence tools

## Pricing Model
- Subscription-based annual licensing
- Pricing based on number of vendors assessed
- Tiered pricing for different feature sets
- Custom enterprise pricing
- Typically $40,000-150,000+ annually
- Volume discounts for large vendor portfolios
- Additional costs for premium monitoring features

## Target Organization Size
- Mid-market to Large Enterprise
- Organizations with 500+ employees
- Healthcare, financial services, retail
- Organizations with significant vendor ecosystems
