# Team Cymru

## Overview
Team Cymru is a specialized threat intelligence research organization providing network flow intelligence, botnet tracking, and internet infrastructure analysis. Founded in 1998, Team Cymru focuses on large-scale internet data collection and analysis, offering both free community services and commercial threat intelligence products for network operators, ISPs, and enterprises.

## Key Features
- NetFlow/IPFIX intelligence analysis
- Botnet and malware C&C tracking
- IP reputation and ASN intelligence
- DDoS attack intelligence
- Pure Signal™ network flow data
- Community services (bogon lists, WHOIS, malware hash registry)
- Internet infrastructure analysis
- Darknet monitoring
- Threat actor infrastructure tracking
- Custom intelligence research

## Pros
- Unique network flow intelligence (NetFlow/IPFIX analysis)
- Valuable free community services
- Long-standing reputation in internet security community
- Excellent for network operators and ISPs

- Large-scale internet visibility

## Cons
