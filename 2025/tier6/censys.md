# Censys

## Overview
Censys is an attack surface management and internet intelligence platform that continuously scans and indexes the internet's devices, services, and certificates. Originally a research project from the University of Michigan, Censys provides organizations with visibility into their external attack surface, identifies unknown assets, and monitors for security risks across internet-exposed infrastructure.

## Key Features
- Continuous internet-wide scanning and indexing
- Attack surface discovery and management
- Unknown and shadow IT asset discovery
- SSL/TLS certificate monitoring
- Cloud asset discovery (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- Host and service enumeration
- Risk scoring and prioritization
- Historical data and change tracking
- Search engine for internet-connected devices
- API access for automated queries

## Pros
- Comprehensive internet scanning and asset discovery
- Excellent for unknown asset and shadow IT detection
- Strong SSL/TLS certificate intelligence
- Good cloud asset discovery capabilities
- User-friendly search interface
- Valuable for attack surface management
- Regular scanning updates (weekly)
- Academic research foundation provides credibility
- Good API for automation
- Free academic access available

## Cons
- Focused on asset discovery vs. threat intelligence
- Limited threat actor or malware intelligence
- Not a comprehensive CTI platform
- Can identify many assets requiring manual triage
- Best for external attack surface vs. internal security
- Some false positives in asset attribution
- Query limits on lower tiers
- Scanning frequency may not meet real-time needs

## Use Cases
- Attack surface management and discovery
- Unknown and shadow IT asset identification
- Cloud asset inventory and monitoring
- SSL/TLS certificate monitoring
- M&A due diligence
- Third-party risk assessment
- Security research and reconnaissance
- External vulnerability management

## Integration Capabilities
- SIEM integrations (via API)
- Threat intelligence platforms (limited)
- Security orchestration tools
- Asset management systems (via API)
- RESTful API with good documentation
- Python SDK
- Webhooks for change notifications
- Limited native security product integration

## Pricing Model
- Free tier with limited searches
- Paid tiers: Pro, Teams, and Enterprise
- Subscription-based annual licensing
- Pricing based on query volume and features
- Transparent pricing starting at ~$200/month for Pro
- Enterprise custom pricing
- Academic and research free access

## Target Organization Size
- Small to Large Enterprise
- Security teams of all sizes
- Organizations managing complex infrastructure
- Cloud-native companies
