# Nisos

## Overview
Nisos is a managed intelligence company that combines technology with human intelligence analysis to provide investigations, threat intelligence, and digital risk protection services. Specializing in complex investigations and adversary-focused intelligence, Nisos offers both technology platform access and managed services for organizations requiring deep investigative capabilities and custom intelligence.

## Key Features
- Managed intelligence services (human analysts)
- Deep and dark web investigations
- Fraud and financial crime intelligence
- Cryptocurrency tracking and analysis
- Threat actor profiling and attribution
- Digital forensics and investigations
- Brand protection and reputation monitoring
- Executive and VIP protection
- Custom intelligence requirements
- Nisos Navigator platform (technology + services)

## Pros
- Strong human intelligence (HUMINT) expertise
- Excellent for complex investigations
- Good cryptocurrency and blockchain analysis
- Deep expertise in fraud and financial crime
- Managed service model provides expert analysis
- Custom intelligence tailored to specific needs
- Strong for high-stakes investigations
- Good law enforcement and government experience
- Flexible engagement models
- Strong in cryptocurrency-related threats

## Cons
- Premium pricing for managed services
- Not a self-service platform like traditional TIPs
- Best suited for specific investigation needs
- Limited technology platform for independent use
- Requires human analyst time (not automated)
- Smaller vendor with limited brand recognition
- Best for episodic investigations vs. continuous monitoring
- Integration with security tools limited

## Use Cases
- Complex fraud and financial crime investigations
- Cryptocurrency theft and scam investigations
- Executive and VIP threat investigations
- M&A due diligence and investigations
- Brand protection and counterfeiting
- Threat actor attribution and profiling
- Digital forensics and incident response
- Legal and litigation support

## Integration Capabilities
- Managed service delivery (reports, briefings)
- Limited platform API access
- Custom data delivery formats
- Integration primarily through analyst deliverables
- Email and secure portal for intelligence sharing
- Case management integration (limited)

## Pricing Model
- Managed service pricing model
- Custom project-based pricing
- Retainer agreements available
- Navigator platform subscription (separate)
- Premium pricing reflects analyst expertise
- Typically $50,000-250,000+ for investigations
- Annual retainers for ongoing intelligence
- No self-service pricing tiers

## Target Organization Size
- Mid-market to Large Enterprise
- Law firms and legal teams
- Financial services and cryptocurrency companies
- Organizations requiring complex investigations
- Government and law enforcement
