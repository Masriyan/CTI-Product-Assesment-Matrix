# Cyberint Argos

## Overview
Cyberint Argos is an external threat intelligence and digital risk protection platform that monitors the open, deep, and dark web for threats targeting an organization's assets, brand, and employees. Founded in Israel, Cyberint combines automated intelligence collection with expert analysis to provide early warning of cyber threats, data leaks, fraud, and brand abuse.

## Key Features
- Argos external threat intelligence platform
- Deep and dark web monitoring
- Phishing and brand impersonation detection
- Credential leak and exposure alerts
- Executive and VIP monitoring
- Threat actor tracking
- Vulnerability and exploit intelligence
- Supply chain and third-party risk monitoring
- Mobile app store monitoring
- Automated alerting and risk scoring

## Pros
- Strong dark web and underground monitoring
- Good credential compromise detection
- Proactive phishing and brand abuse alerts
- User-friendly platform with clear prioritization
- Expert analyst support and managed services
- Fast alerting on critical threats

## Cons

## Integration Capabilities
- SIEM integrations (Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, QRadar)
- Threat intelligence platforms (Anomali, ThreatConnect, MISP)
- SOAR platforms (limited)
- Ticketing systems (ServiceNow, Jira)
- RESTful API
- Webhook and email notifications
- Slack and Teams integration
- STIX/TAXII support (limited)

## Pricing Model
- Subscription-based annual licensing
- Tiered pricing based on modules and organization size
- Managed service options available
- Custom enterprise pricing
- Competitive mid-market pricing ($30,000-80,000 annually)
- Volume discounts for larger deployments

## Target Organization Size
- Small to Mid-market Enterprise

- Financial services, healthcare, retail
