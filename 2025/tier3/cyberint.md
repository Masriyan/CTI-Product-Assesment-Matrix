The platform's automated monitoring detects organization-specific threats including credential leaks from stealer malware, corporate data exposure in breach databases, phishing domains targeting customers, executive information compiled for social engineering, brand impersonation on social media, and threat actor discussions about targeting specific industries or organizations.
