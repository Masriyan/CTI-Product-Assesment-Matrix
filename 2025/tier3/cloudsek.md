# CloudSEK XVigil

## Overview
CloudSEK is an AI-powered digital risk monitoring platform headquartered in Singapore and India. The XVigil platform provides real-time threat intelligence from the surface, deep, and dark web, monitoring for data leaks, brand abuse, infrastructure vulnerabilities, and emerging threats. CloudSEK specializes in making external threat intelligence actionable through AI-powered analysis and prioritization.

## Key Features
- XVigil AI-powered threat intelligence platform
- Deep and dark web monitoring
- Data leak and breach detection
- Brand and domain monitoring
- Attack surface discovery and management
- Vulnerability intelligence
- Threat actor tracking
- Supply chain risk monitoring
- Ransomware intelligence
- AI-powered threat prioritization (BeVigil for mobile)
