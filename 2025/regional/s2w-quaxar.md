# S2W QUAXAR (South Korea)

## Overview
S2W is a South Korean cybersecurity company providing threat intelligence and digital risk protection through its QUAXAR platform. Specializing in APAC region threats with particular expertise in North Korean threat actors, S2W offers deep and dark web monitoring, malware analysis, and targeted attack intelligence focused on threats relevant to Korean and regional organizations.

## Key Features
- QUAXAR threat intelligence platform
- Deep and dark web monitoring
- North Korean threat actor expertise (Lazarus, Kimsuky, etc.)
- APT and targeted attack intelligence
- Cryptocurrency theft and fraud intelligence
- Ransomware intelligence
- Korean and Chinese underground monitoring
- Malware analysis and reverse engineering
- Brand and digital risk protection
- APAC region-specific threat intelligence
