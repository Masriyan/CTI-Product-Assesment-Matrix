# ThreatBook (China)

## Overview
ThreatBook (微步在线) is a leading Chinese threat intelligence company providing comprehensive threat intelligence, security analytics, and incident response services. With deep expertise in Chinese threat landscape and APT groups, ThreatBook offers both cloud-based and on-premise threat intelligence platforms serving Chinese enterprises, government agencies, and organizations operating in Greater China.

## Key Features
- Threat intelligence cloud platform (X社区)
- APT threat intelligence (China-focused)
- Malware analysis and sandbox
- Threat hunting platform
- Dark web monitoring (Chinese underground)
- Vulnerability intelligence
- Threat actor tracking and profiling
- Automated threat detection and response
- Integration with Chinese security products
- Local threat intelligence feeds
