# Analyst1

## Overview
Analyst1 is a threat intelligence platform focused on operationalizing intelligence through evidence-based analysis and adversary profiling. The platform emphasizes connecting disparate intelligence sources to provide contextualized threat actor intelligence, enabling security teams to understand not just what threats exist, but who is behind them and why they matter to their organization.

## Key Features
- Evidence-based threat actor profiling
- Adversary infrastructure tracking
- Malware family analysis and tracking
- Attack pattern and TTP documentation
- Intelligence graph for relationship mapping
- Validated intelligence with evidence trails
- MITRE ATT&CK framework integration
- Indicator enrichment and contextualization
- Threat hunting capabilities
- Custom intelligence feeds

## Pros
- Strong focus on threat actor attribution and profiling
- Evidence-based intelligence reduces false positives
- Good contextualization of threats
- User-friendly interface with intuitive navigation
- Effective relationship mapping and visualization
- Competitive pricing for mid-market
- Good integration with common security tools
- Regular intelligence updates and research
- Strong customer support
- Fast search and query capabilities

## Cons
- Smaller vendor with limited brand recognition
- Integration ecosystem less extensive than major platforms
- Intelligence coverage less comprehensive than Tier 1 vendors
- Newer platform with shorter track record
- Community and user base smaller
- Some advanced features still developing
- Limited dark web monitoring compared to specialized vendors

## Use Cases
- Threat actor research and attribution
- Threat hunting operations
- Incident response and forensic analysis
- SOC analyst investigations
- Malware family tracking
- Vulnerability and exploit intelligence
- Strategic threat intelligence programs
- Red team and purple team operations

## Integration Capabilities
- SIEM integrations (Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, Chronicle)
- Threat intelligence platforms (Anomali, ThreatConnect, MISP)
- SOAR platforms (limited)
- EDR/XDR integration (limited)
- RESTful API
- STIX/TAXII support
- Webhook and email notifications
- Integration ecosystem growing

## Pricing Model
- Subscription-based annual licensing
- Tiered pricing based on users and features
- Custom enterprise pricing
- Competitive mid-market pricing ($30,000-70,000 annually)
- Professional services available
- Training included with subscription
- Multi-year discounts available

## Target Organization Size
- Small to Mid-market Enterprise
- Organizations with 500-5,000 employees
- Security teams with 5-50 analysts
- Growing enterprise customer base
