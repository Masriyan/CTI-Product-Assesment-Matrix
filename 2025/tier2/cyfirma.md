# CYFIRMA DeCYFIR

## Overview
CYFIRMA is an external threat landscape management platform that combines threat intelligence, digital risk protection, and attack surface management. The DeCYFIR platform uses AI and machine learning to provide predictive cyber intelligence, helping organizations understand their external threat landscape and anticipate attacks before they occur.

## Key Features
- Predictive cyber threat intelligence
- External attack surface discovery and monitoring
- Deep and dark web monitoring
- Threat actor tracking and targeting analysis
- Industry-specific threat intelligence
- Vulnerability and exploit intelligence
- Supply chain and third-party risk monitoring
- Brand protection and digital risk monitoring
- Early warning system for targeted attacks
- MITRE ATT&CK framework mapping

## Pros
- Focus on predictive and targeted threat intelligence
- Good coverage of APAC region threats
- External attack surface management capabilities
- Industry-specific intelligence (healthcare, finance, manufacturing)
- Proactive threat actor targeting analysis
- User-friendly platform interface
- Good value proposition for mid-market
- Growing dark web monitoring capabilities
- Strong customer support
- Regular intelligence reports and briefings

## Cons
- Smaller brand recognition compared to Tier 1 vendors
- Integration ecosystem still developing
- Limited presence outside APAC and select markets
- Analyst team smaller than major vendors
- Some customers report intelligence depth varies by region
- API documentation could be more comprehensive
- Newer platform with limited long-term track record
