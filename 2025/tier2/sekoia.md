# Sekoia.io

## Overview
Sekoia.io is a European cybersecurity company offering a comprehensive XDR platform with integrated threat intelligence capabilities. Founded in France, Sekoia.io combines Security Operations Center (SOC) automation, extended detection and response (XDR), and threat intelligence in a unified platform, with strong focus on European threats and regulatory compliance (GDPR, NIS2).

## Key Features
- Integrated XDR and threat intelligence platform
- Threat intelligence center (TIC) with curated feeds
- SOC automation and case management
- MITRE ATT&CK framework integration
- Threat hunting capabilities
- Malware analysis and sandbox
- Dark web monitoring
- Automated detection rules and playbooks
- European threat landscape focus
- GDPR and NIS2 compliance features

## Pros
- Unified XDR and threat intelligence platform
- Strong focus on European threat landscape and compliance
- Good for organizations requiring GDPR compliance
