The SOCRadar XTI platform automatically discovers and monitors an organization's external-facing attack surface‚Äîincluding domains, subdomains, IP addresses, cloud assets, SSL certificates, and exposed services‚Äîcontinuously scanning for misconfigurations, vulnerabilities, and security exposures. The platform's Dark Web & Cyber Threat Intelligence module monitors 1,000+ underground sources for organization-specific threats: leaked credentials, corporate data for sale, ransomware targeting, and brand abuse. SOCRadar's competitive pricing, rapid deployment, and focus on actionable external intelligence have made it particularly popular among mid-market organizations and MSSPs seeking comprehensive external threat visibility without the complexity and cost of enterprise TIP platforms.
