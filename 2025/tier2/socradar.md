# SOCRadar Extended Threat Intelligence (XTI)

## Overview
SOCRadar is a digital risk protection and extended threat intelligence platform that combines threat intelligence, attack surface management, and brand protection capabilities. Founded in Turkey with global operations, SOCRadar provides organizations with visibility into their external threat landscape, including dark web monitoring, vulnerability intelligence, and third-party risk assessment.

## Key Features
- Extended Threat Intelligence (XTI) platform
- Dark web and underground monitoring
- Attack surface discovery and management
- Brand and digital asset monitoring
- Vulnerability intelligence and prioritization
- Threat actor tracking
- Data leak detection
- Third-party and supply chain risk monitoring
- Phishing and domain monitoring
- MITRE ATT&CK framework integration

## Pros
- Comprehensive external threat visibility
- Good attack surface management capabilities
- Strong dark web monitoring
- User-friendly dashboard and alerts
- Competitive pricing for SMB and mid-market
- Fast deployment and time-to-value
- Regular threat intelligence reports
- Good vulnerability prioritization

## Cons

- API capabilities moderate

## Pricing Model
- Subscription-based annual licensing

- Custom enterprise pricing
