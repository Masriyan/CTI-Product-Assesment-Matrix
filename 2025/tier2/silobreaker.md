# Silobreaker

## Overview
Silobreaker is a threat intelligence aggregation and analysis platform that combines open-source intelligence (OSINT), dark web data, and commercial threat feeds into a unified interface. Founded in Sweden in 2009, Silobreaker focuses on enabling security analysts to quickly discover, analyze, and visualize threat intelligence from thousands of sources using advanced search and relationship mapping.

## Key Features
- Aggregation of 100,000+ intelligence sources
- Advanced search and filtering capabilities
- Relationship mapping and visualization
- Geopolitical and cyber threat intelligence
- Dark web and underground forum monitoring
- Threat actor and campaign tracking
- Automated intelligence reports and briefings
- MITRE ATT&CK integration
- Trend analysis and emerging threat detection
- Custom intelligence dashboards

## Pros
- Extensive source aggregation (OSINT, commercial, dark web)
- Powerful search and discovery capabilities
- Excellent visualization and relationship mapping
- Good geopolitical and strategic intelligence
- User-friendly interface for analysts
- Quick time-to-value for intelligence research
- Regular content updates from diverse sources
- Good for strategic and operational intelligence
- Competitive pricing
- Strong European presence and customer base

## Cons
- Primarily an aggregation platform (not original research)
- Integration with security tools limited compared to TIPs
- Quality varies based on aggregated sources
- Less tactical IOC feeds compared to specialized vendors
- API capabilities moderate
- Best suited for analyst research vs. automated operations
- Some customers report information overload requiring tuning

## Use Cases
- Strategic threat intelligence research
- Geopolitical risk monitoring
- Threat actor and campaign research
- Executive threat briefings

- Brand and reputation monitoring
- Supply chain risk assessment
- Security analyst research and investigation

## Integration Capabilities
- SIEM integration (limited)
- Threat intelligence platform integration (limited)

- Email and PDF report distribution
- Slack and Teams integration
- RSS feeds
- CSV and JSON exports
- Integration primarily through data export vs. bidirectional
