# Group-IB Threat Intelligence

## Overview
Group-IB is a Singapore-based cybersecurity company specializing in preventing cyberattacks and investigating high-tech crimes and online fraud. Founded in Russia in 2003 and relocated to Singapore, Group-IB provides threat intelligence with strong focus on APAC, EMEA, and CIS regions, offering deep expertise in APT tracking, cybercrime investigation, and financial fraud prevention.

## Key Features
- Threat Intelligence & Attribution platform
- High-tech crime investigation capabilities
- APT and targeted attack intelligence
- Financial fraud and banking trojan monitoring
- Ransomware intelligence
- Dark web and underground monitoring
- Brand protection and anti-phishing
- Compromised card monitoring
- Threat hunting platform
- Digital risk protection
