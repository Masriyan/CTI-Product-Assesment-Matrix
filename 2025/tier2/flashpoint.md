# Flashpoint

## Overview
Flashpoint is a Business Risk Intelligence (BRI) platform that specializes in monitoring illicit online communities, including deep and dark web forums, chat services, and paste sites. Founded in 2010, Flashpoint combines automated collection with human intelligence to provide organizations with early warnings about cyber threats, fraud, and physical security risks.

## Key Features
- Illicit community monitoring (forums, chat, marketplaces)
- Deep and dark web intelligence collection
- Finished intelligence reports and analysis
- Vulnerability intelligence (VulnDB acquisition)
- Brand and executive monitoring
- Threat actor profiling
- Technical data feeds (IOCs, malware, exploits)
- Physical security and geopolitical threat intelligence
- Fraud and financial crime intelligence
- ChatGPT-style AI search (Copilot)

## Pros
- Extensive coverage of underground communities and forums
- Human intelligence (HUMINT) expertise
- Strong context and finished intelligence reporting
- Comprehensive vulnerability database (VulnDB)
- Good coverage of fraud and financial threats
- Early warning of emerging threats and campaigns
- Physical security and geopolitical intelligence
- User-friendly platform interface
- AI-powered search and analysis (Flashpoint Copilot)
- Strong analyst support team

## Cons
- Premium pricing for comprehensive features
- Best suited for organizations facing diverse threats
- Some overlap between cyber and physical security focus
- Integration ecosystem limited compared to pure CTI platforms
- Requires analyst time to process intelligence
- VulnDB integration still maturing post-acquisition
- API capabilities moderate

## Use Cases
- Brand protection and reputation monitoring
- Executive and VIP protection
- Fraud detection and prevention
- Third-party and supply chain risk monitoring
- Vulnerability management and patch prioritization
- Financial crime investigation
- Ransomware and malware intelligence
- Geopolitical risk assessment
- M&A due diligence

## Integration Capabilities
- SIEM integrations (Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, QRadar)
- Threat intelligence platforms (Anomali, ThreatConnect, ThreatQuotient)
- SOAR platforms (limited)
- RESTful API
- STIX/TAXII feeds
- Slack and Microsoft Teams integration
- Webhook notifications
- CSV and JSON exports

## Pricing Model
- Subscription-based annual licensing
- Tiered pricing based on modules and organization size
- Collections (technical intelligence) and Finished Intelligence (separate modules)
- VulnDB pricing separate or bundled
- Custom enterprise pricing
- Typically starts at $50,000+ annually
- Additional costs for premium analyst support

## Target Organization Size
- Mid-market to Large Enterprise
- Financial services, retail, and e-commerce
- Organizations with 500+ employees
- Government and law enforcement agencies
