# Cyware CTIX

## Overview
Cyware Cyber Threat Intelligence eXchange (CTIX) is a threat intelligence platform designed for automated threat intelligence operations and cross-organizational intelligence sharing. Part of the Cyware Cyber Fusion suite, CTIX focuses on enabling organizations to aggregate, enrich, analyze, and share threat intelligence bidirectionally across teams, tools, and communities.

## Key Features
- Automated threat intelligence aggregation from multiple sources
- Bidirectional intelligence sharing
- Threat intelligence enrichment and scoring
- TAXII server and client capabilities
- Integration with Cyware Fusion and Response (CFTR) SOAR
- Threat actor and campaign tracking
- Custom intelligence feeds and watchlists
- MITRE ATT&CK integration
- Indicator lifecycle management
- Community and ISAC intelligence sharing

## Pros
- Strong focus on intelligence sharing and collaboration
- Good integration with Cyware SOAR platform
- Automated enrichment and normalization
- User-friendly interface

## Integration Capabilities
- Native integration with Cyware Fusion and Response (SOAR)
- SIEM integrations (Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, QRadar, Chronicle)
- EDR/XDR platforms (CrowdStrike, SentinelOne, Microsoft Defender)
- Firewalls and network security (Palo Alto, Cisco, Fortinet)
- Threat intelligence platforms (MISP, OpenCTI)
- Ticketing systems (ServiceNow, Jira)
- RESTful API
- STIX/TAXII 2.x compliant
- 100+ pre-built integrations
