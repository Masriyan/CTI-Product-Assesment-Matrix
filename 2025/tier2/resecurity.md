# Resecurity Context

## Overview
Resecurity is a cybersecurity intelligence and protection company offering the Context platform for threat intelligence, dark web monitoring, and digital risk protection. Founded in 2016, Resecurity specializes in adversary-centric intelligence, credential compromise detection, and financial crime prevention, with strong expertise in underground cybercrime ecosystems.
