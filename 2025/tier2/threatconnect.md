# ThreatConnect

## Overview
ThreatConnect is a comprehensive Threat Intelligence Platform that combines threat intelligence management, orchestration, analytics, and automation. Founded in 2011, ThreatConnect pioneered the concept of intelligence-driven security operations, providing organizations with a platform to aggregate, enrich, analyze, and operationalize threat intelligence at scale.

## Key Features
- Collective Analytics Intelligence (CAL) for collaborative intelligence
- Threat intelligence aggregation and management
- Playbook automation and orchestration
- Case management and incident response
- Diamond Model and Kill Chain analytics
- MITRE ATT&CK framework integration
- Threat scoring and prioritization
- Intelligence source management
- Custom indicator and entity types
- Adversary and campaign tracking

## Pros
- Comprehensive all-in-one TIP and orchestration platform
- Strong community intelligence sharing (CAL)
- Excellent automation and orchestration capabilities
- User-friendly interface with good visualization
- Flexible data model for custom use cases
- Good integration ecosystem
- Strong for both tactical and strategic intelligence
- Active development and feature releases
- Good for building intelligence-driven security programs
- Effective collaboration features

## Cons
- Can be complex to fully configure and optimize
- Pricing at premium tier for full features
- Learning curve for advanced analytics features
- Some overlap between modules can cause confusion
- Resource requirements can be high for large deployments
- Community edition has limited functionality
- Custom playbook development requires training

## Use Cases
- Intelligence-driven security operations
- SOC threat intelligence management
- Threat hunting and investigation
- Incident response orchestration
- Vulnerability prioritization with threat context
- Intelligence sharing in industry groups (ISACs)
- Strategic threat intelligence programs
- Red team and adversary simulation planning

## Integration Capabilities
- SIEM integrations (Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, Chronicle, QRadar)
- Native orchestration (ThreatConnect Playbooks)
- EDR/XDR platforms (CrowdStrike, Microsoft Defender, Carbon Black)
- Firewalls (Palo Alto, Cisco, Fortinet)
- Ticketing (ServiceNow, Jira, Remedy)
- Threat intelligence sources (Recorded Future, Anomali, commercial feeds)
- RESTful API with SDKs (Python, JavaScript, Java)
- STIX/TAXII client and server
- MISP integration
- 200+ pre-built integrations

## Pricing Model
- Subscription-based annual licensing
- Tiered editions (Team, Enterprise, Threat Intelligence Platform)
- Pricing based on users, integrations, and data volume
- Community edition available (limited features)
- Custom enterprise pricing
- Professional services for implementation
- Training and certification programs
- Pricing typically starts at $50,000+ annually for commercial editions

## Target Organization Size
- Mid-market to Large Enterprise
- Government and defense contractors
- Financial services and critical infrastructure
- Organizations with 500+ employees
- Suitable for mature security operations teams
